# Privacy Policy

Welcome to Threadseeker! (referred to as "the Site," "we," or "our"). This Privacy Policy governs your use of [https://threadseeker.app](https://threadseeker.app) and explains how we collect, protect, and disclose data generated through your use of our services. We use your data to provide and improve the services we offer.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. 

Unless otherwise defined in this Privacy Policy, the terms used herein have the same meanings as in our Terms and Conditions.

## Services

"Services" refer to the Threadseeker website, as well as other features provided by Threadseeker, including account and post data analytics tools. 

"Queried Entities" refer to the Threads accounts analyzed via this Site. Threads is a service provided by [Threads.net](https://threads.net).

"Personal Data" refers to data that can identify an individual, either on its own or when combined with other information available to us.

"Usage Data" refers to data automatically collected when you interact with our services (e.g., time spent on pages visited).

"Data Controller" means a natural or legal person who (alone or jointly with others) determines the purposes and means of processing personal data. For this Privacy Policy, we are the Data Controller of your data.

"Data Processor" (or Service Provider) refers to any natural or legal person who processes data on behalf of the Data Controller. We may use third-party service providers to process your data more effectively.

"User" refers to the individual using our services. The User corresponds to the data subject who is the subject of personal data.

"Queried User" refers to individuals whose Threads accounts are queried and analyzed on this Site. Threads is a service provided by [Threads.net](https://threads.net).

## Data Collection and Use

We collect various types of data to provide and improve our services, including:

- Public data from Queried Threads accounts.
- Usage Data.
- Cookies and Tracking Data.

### Public Data from Queried Threads Accounts

If you are a Queried User, or if you query another individual's public Threads account via this Site, we may collect public account data, including but not limited to:

- Account username.
- Full name.
- Account ID.
- Follower count.
- Up to 25 most recent publicly posted threads.

We may analyze this data internally to enhance service experience and improve statistical accuracy.

### Usage Data

When you access our services via a browser or mobile device, we may collect information sent by your browser ("Usage Data").

Usage Data may include:

- Your IP address.
- Browser type and version.
- Pages of our service visited.
- Time and date of your visit.
- Time spent on those pages.
- Unique device identifiers and diagnostic data.

When accessing the service via a mobile device, Usage Data may include:

- Your mobile device type.
- Unique mobile device ID.
- IP address.
- Mobile operating system and browser type.
- Diagnostic data.

### Cookies and Tracking Data

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to monitor activities on our services and retain certain information.

Cookies are small files stored on your device that may include an anonymous unique identifier. Tracking technologies like beacons, tags, and scripts help collect and analyze data to improve our services.

You can configure your browser to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. However, some features of our services may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

## Data Usage and Management

We use collected data for various purposes, including:

- Providing and improving our analytics services.
- Enabling interactive features when you choose to participate.
- Providing customer support.
- Gathering valuable insights to improve our services.
- Monitoring service usage.
- Detecting, preventing, and addressing technical issues.
- For any other purpose disclosed at the time you provide information.
- With your consent, for any other purpose.

You understand and agree that your data may be shared with our third-party partners for business needs without prior notice.

## Data Retention

We retain your Personal Data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. We will retain and use your data as required to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Usage Data is generally retained for a shorter period unless used to enhance service security or functionality, or required by law.

## Data Disclosure

We may disclose your data:

- To comply with legal obligations or valid requests by authorities.
- To protect the rights, property, or safety of the Site, our users, or others.

## Data Security

Whether you are a User or a Queried User, the security of your data is important to us. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## Links to Other Websites

Our services may contain links to third-party websites. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to their site. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

We assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of third-party sites or services.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
